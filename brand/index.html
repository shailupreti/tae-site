Who We Are

A critical piece in the American energy value chain.

The American Energy champions an all-of-the-above domestic energy strategy — solar, nuclear, natural gas, wind, hydro, geothermal, and storage. We don't build the entire system, and we don't claim to. Our critical, irreplaceable piece is the battery: from battery metals, through battery-grade refining and American-made cells, to grid storage — the link that makes an all-of-the-above grid actually reliable.

The Mark

The mark is an ascending bar climb rising into a cyan growth streak, tipped with an amber spark — momentum, American energy, and the upward trajectory of independence. The navy tile version is the app/favicon form.

Usage Rules
Do keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the bolt mark. Place on deep navy or white.
Do use the on-dark version over navy/photography and the on-light version over white/light surfaces.
Don't recolor the wordmark, stretch it, add shadows, or rotate the mark.
Don't place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds or busy imagery without a scrim.
