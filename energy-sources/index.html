America's Energy Portfolio

No single source powers a modern nation. The American Energy advances every domestic resource — firm and renewable, generation and storage — built on a traceable American supply chain from critical minerals to megawatts.

Seven Pillars

An Integrated American Energy Mix.

Firm baseload — nuclear, natural gas, hydro, and geothermal — provides the always-on backbone. Solar and wind add low-cost, clean generation. Battery storage ties it all together, turning variable output into firm, on-demand power. Together they deliver energy security at home.
