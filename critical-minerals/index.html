Why It Matters

The Supply Chain Gap Is America's Greatest Battery Risk.

Over 70% of global battery-grade mineral processing runs through China today. From lithium carbonate to synthetic graphite to refined cobalt, America's battery future depends on Chinese processing at nearly every step. The American Energy exists to change that — mineral by mineral, step by step.

Partner With Us

Processing Gap

Even minerals mined in the US are often shipped to China for processing before returning as battery-grade materials.

Traceability Deficit

Most battery supply chains today cannot trace materials back to their mine of origin — a critical gap for defense and DoD applications.

Recycling Opportunity

Domestic recycling of spent batteries creates a secondary supply loop that reduces dependence on primary mining and overseas processing.
