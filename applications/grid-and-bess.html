Grid-Scale Energy Storage

Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) are the fastest-growing segment of the global energy market. As variable renewable generation displaces dispatchable fossil generation, grid operators need storage to balance supply and demand, provide frequency regulation, and ensure grid stability.

NPT Cell-to-Container Architecture

Near Prime Technologies' cell-to-container BESS scales from C4V's 53Ah BMLMP prismatic cell through 8S3P modules (4.7 kWh), trays (9.4 kWh), racks (188.2 kWh), to 15-rack ISO containers (2.82 MWh). Multiple containers aggregate to any project scale — from behind-the-meter commercial installations to gigawatt-hour utility deployments.

IRA Section 48 ITC: 30% base Investment Tax Credit for battery storage, increasing to 40% with domestic content adder — requiring verified US-sourced minerals and components. The American Energy maps qualifying supply chain configurations.

Key Supply Chain Requirements

  • LFP or BMLMP prismatic cells from American-designed manufacturing
  • Iron and phosphorus from domestic US sources
  • American BMS with full Digital DNA traceability
  • Container integration with American steel fabricators
