Home and Building Energy Storage

Residential and commercial battery storage keeps a family's power on during a hurricane, reduces a small business's electricity bill, and stores solar energy for after-sunset use. LFP is the dominant chemistry for home and building storage — its inherent thermal stability makes it the safest option for indoor installation, and its 3,000–5,000+ cycle life means daily cycling for 10–15 years without significant degradation.

IRA 30% Investment Tax Credit: The Inflation Reduction Act provides a 30% federal tax credit for residential battery storage systems (Section 25D), dramatically accelerating residential storage adoption and creating demand for domestic battery products.

Virtual Power Plant Participation

Aggregated residential storage systems can participate in Virtual Power Plant (VPP) programs — grid operator and utility programs dispatching aggregated distributed storage to provide peak shaving, frequency regulation, and demand response. American homes with domestic batteries become energy infrastructure contributors.

Key Supply Chain Requirements

  • LFP chemistry using domestic iron and phosphate sources
  • UL 9540 and UL 9540A certified system integration
  • Solar coupling compatible with major US inverter brands
  • 10-year warranty capability from domestic cell manufacturers
