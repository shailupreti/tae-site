The C4V BMLMP prismatic reference cell — the foundation of Near Prime Technologies' cell-to-container BESS architecture and The American Energy's flagship commercial cell.

  • 53 Ah capacity, 3.7V nominal, 196 Wh
  • Operating window: 2.8V – 4.1V
  • Forced protection: 2.7V (over-discharge) / 4.2V (max charge)
  • Continuous charge/discharge ≤1C, peak ≤2C
