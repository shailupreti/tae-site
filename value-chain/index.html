Where we fit: The American Energy doesn't build every part of this chain — and doesn't claim to. America's energy system is too vast for any one company. Our critical, irreplaceable piece is the battery: from battery metals and battery-grade refining through American-made cells to grid storage. That is the link that makes an all-of-the-above grid actually reliable.

Traceability throughout: Digital DNA — C4V's materials-genomics and supply-chain platform — anchors a verifiable record at every stage, from the geological source of a mineral to the operational history of a deployed battery. It is how an all-of-the-above value chain becomes auditable for defense, IRA domestic-content, and ESG requirements.
